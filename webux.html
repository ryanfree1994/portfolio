Website UX Evaluation

Livonia, Michigan

In winter 2017, I was a member of a student consulting team that evaluated the usability of Schoolcraft College's website. When we met with the IT department at Schoolcraft College, we were informed that end users were frustrated with the navigation, content, and layout of the website. With this problem statement in mind, we developed a user-centered research and evaluation strategy that would provide solutions to these problem areas. Over the course of four months, my team and I were able to complete the following methodologies:

  • Interviews
  • Affinity Diagramming
  • Personas/Scenarios
  • Interaction Mapping
  • Comparative Analysis
  • Surveys
  • Quantitative Analysis
  • Heuristic Evaluations
  • Usability Testing
