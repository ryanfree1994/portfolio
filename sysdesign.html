System Design

Luanda, Angola

The Angolan Ministry of Health has been working with USAID and PSM to design a supply chain for medical products that will be monitored through the usage of an electronic logistics management information system (eLMIS). During my involvement on the project, I assisted in requirements gathering/definition and validatiaton for the eLMIS by co-hosting workshops with key stakeholders from the Angolan Ministry of Health and Provincial warehouses.

The two workshops I co-hosted occurred in Luanda, Angola during October 2018 and January 2019. My responsibilites included development of training materials - which were centered around participatory research principles - and configuration of the demo eLMIS. While my time dedicated to the project has decreased, I am still in invovled throughout the design of use cases, personas/scenarios, and monitoring and evaluation of the eLMIS. The following skills were used:

  • Participatory Research
  • Open Card Sorting
  • Validation Matrix
  • Workshop Design
  • Personas/Scenarios
  • Monitoring and Evaluation
