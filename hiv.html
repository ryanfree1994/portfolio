HIV/AIDS Prevention Research

Southeastern Michigan

From 2015-2018, I was involved in HIV/AIDS preventative research at the University of Michigan School of Public Health. I assisted in manuscript development/submission, qualitative and quantitative analysis, mHealth intervention development, and grant/program proposals. WIth this experience, I have had the privilege of being first author on two academic manuscripts and co-author on three manuscripts. I also had the opportunity to present at the Population Association of America Conference in March 2016. If you would like to read any of my articles, please reach out via the Contact page. During my time as a Research Assistant, I became adept in the following methodologies:

  • Literature Reviews
  • Interviewing
  • Qualitative Analysis
  • Manuscript Development & Submission
  • Quantitative Analysis
  • Peer Reviews
  • Grant Proposal Development
  • Survey Construction
  • Focus Group Discussions
